TOUR OVERVIEW

4 DAYS LAKE NAIVASHA AND MASAI MARA

This Safari takes you to Lake Naivasha and Masai Mara National Reserve – Kenya’s finest and most unique wildlife sanctuary.
Day one
Our driver-guide will pick you up after breakfast. You will depart by road for Lake Naivasha. You will have a brief stopover at the Great Rift valley viewpoint to catch breathtaking views of the rift valley. You will arrive at your lodge in time for lunch; later, take a boat ride to Crescent Island. Many different types of animals are found on the Island including, Giraffes, waterbucks, wildebeests, Gazelles, Impala, Dik diks which can be seen during the walk. Later you will retire back at our cozy accommodations in Naivasha. Meals and accommodation at Lake Naivasha Lodge. Meals: Lunch & Dinner
Day two

We depart Naivasha after breakfast and drive through the dramatic Great Rift Valley to the Masai Mara Game Reserve this morning. This enormous reserve is part of the vast Serengeti plains, famously known for its spectacular great wildebeest migration. You will have lunch at the Camp and relax before departing for the afternoon game drive. Later return to the Camp for dinner and overnight.

Dinner and overnight stay at Enkorok Camp

Meals: Breakfast Lunch and Dinner

Day three

Today we spend the day in the Masai Mara Game Reserve searching for the big cats of the Masai Mara. All “Big Five” animals (elephants, lions, leopards, rhinos, and buffalo) can be spotted here. Herds of plains, zebras, and wildebeests are found throughout the park. Other animals include giraffes, jackals, gnus, warthogs, gazelles, hartebeests, hyenas, bat-eared foxes, Topi antelope, hippos, and crocodiles in the Mara River. The Mara birds also come in every color and size. More than 400 bird species have already been recorded, including birds of prey. You will have a picnic lunch in the park and return to your Camp for dinner and overnight.

Budget Accommodation: Enkorok Camp

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner

Day 4: Masai Mara Game Reserve – Drive to Nairobi

If you are not participating in the hot air balloon (optional), enjoy morning tea/ coffee or Hot chocolate followed by a morning game drive. On the plains are enormous herds of grazing animals plus the elusive Cheetah and leopard hiding amidst acacia trees. Return to the lodge/ camp for breakfast. Optional visit to an authentic Masai village (at an extra cost) to witness the singing and dancing part of their daily lives and sacred rituals. Depart the Mara by road back to Nairobi, arriving late afternoon. You will be dropped at your hotel or be transferred to the airport to catch your flight back home.

Meals: Breakfast and lunch

*End of Safari*
