TOUR OVERVIEW

3 Days Amboseli National Park Safari

This is a popular choice for a safari. Amboseli National Park is the exemplary African image of a massive herd of elephants walking in front of the snow-capped peaks of Mt Kilimanjaro. While elephants are the main attraction, there is plenty of other wildlife. The open terrain makes for easy viewing, and the central wetlands make the park an excellent birding hot spot. The park’s relatively small size makes it easy to explore on a 3-day tour
Day one
Pick up in Nairobi in the morning and drive to Amboseli National Park. You will arrive at Kibo Safari Camp in time for lunch. You will check-in and have lunch, and you will enjoy swimming or have a short rest before your afternoon game drive starts. Explore the park and see large herds of elephants and other game animals like zebras, giraffes, and many more. You will also enjoy clear views of Mount Kilimanjaro, which provides a beautiful background (coming out of the clouds during sunset and sunrise). Later return to the camp for dinner and overnight stay. Dinner and overnight at Kibo Safari Camp. Meals: Lunch and Dinner
Day two

Our early morning game drive is most rewarding - it offers the opportunity to find predators either hunting or feeding on their prey. You will have your picnic lunch in the park and enjoy the park as you have unlimited game drives throughout the day. Later return to your camp for dinner and overnight stay.

Dinner and overnight at Kibo Safari Camp.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

Day three

You will have an early morning game drive with the last chance to get a glimpse of Mount Kilimanjaro. Return to the camp for late breakfast, check out and drive back to Nairobi. You will arrive in Nairobi in the afternoon, and you will be dropped at your hotel/residence or the airport to catch your flight.

No accommodation

Meals: Breakfast
